Native iOS Ecosystem

Built with SwiftUI from the ground up. Deep integrations with Files, iCloud, Face ID, and Shortcuts mean HashSign fits seamlessly into your daily workflow.

Every signed contract is cryptographically hashed with SHA-256 and anchored to the Hedera public ledger — creating a tamper-proof, immutable audit trail.

  • Court-admissible timestamp proofs
